User Guide

Welcome to Artrella! This app allows you to chat with Chat GPT and create images using our powerful image generator.

Getting Started

To get started, download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Create an account or log in if you already have one. Explore the chat feature to connect with Chat GPT or try out our image generator to create stunning visuals.

Chat Feature

Our chat feature allows you to connect with Chat GPT. Simply select the chat option and start your conversation.

Image Generator

To create images, navigate to the image generator section. You can input your ideas, and our AI will create images based on your descriptions. Save or share your creations directly from the app.

Can I export my chat history?

Currently, exporting chat history is not supported, but we are working on this feature for future updates.

How do I save the images I create?

After generating an image, you can save it to your device by clicking the "Save" button.
